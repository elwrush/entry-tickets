Write the letter of the correct definition

positive

  1. A. feeling good and happy about something
  2. B. real and true, not imagined or guessed
  3. C. certain to happen; impossible to avoid
10 seconds. Answer: A (feeling good and happy about something)

True or False? Write T or F.

  1. 5. The following is a complete sentence:
    "Because she was tired after the long journey."
  2. 6. This is a run-on sentence:
    "I love reading books I could spend all day in a library."
15 seconds. 5=F (This is a sentence fragment (dependent clause). A complete sentence needs an independent clause.); 6=T (Two independent clauses joined without punctuation or a conjunction — this is a fused sentence (run-on).)

7. Sentence or fragment?
"Although the movie was long and boring."

15 seconds. Answer: fragment
